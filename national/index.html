Determine Company Exposure

Determine in what sectors your business is at risk.

Disruption in any of these sectors can pose a risk to your business, even if your enterprise only creates products and services in one. Further, changes in one sector can affect the quantity or quality of another (e.g. a lack of water can negatively impact power generation). Thus, it is important for your business to take a holistic look at each sector individually and in connection with each other. The sectors covered in these guidelines include:

➢ Energy
➢ Water
➢ Food
➢ Infrastructure
➢ Health

First, look for data that reflect changes or directly report on conditions for each sector. This data will not be available for your business or likely your town or immediate community. However, there is data available for many of these sectors at the state and national level:

1. Direct Data

Find national sector data here: www.smeadaptation.org/national/sector
Find state sector data here: www.smeadaptation.org/state/sector

Find the data you need? How is Mexico performing, how is your state performing on each sector? Depending on the extent of your supply chain, you will need to examine conditions at several geographic scales.
