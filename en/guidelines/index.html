5 Strategy

Several strategies exist according to the vulnerability or opportunity present in each sector. Four key strategies include:

  • Investments in services or products - the SME can seek to expand in its current market by identifying specific services and products that can help communities adapt. Much of this will require new financial investments.

  • Change in internal practices - creating more efficient processes within the business that save energy, water and other resources. Some processes may require little capital investment while others may require installation of new machinery and technology.

  • Collaboration - reaching out to NGOs, other businesses and the government to develop solutions.

  • Gather information - there still may be no apparent path forward. New technologies and approaches may be needed. Check back with smeadaptation.org for solutions.

Depending on the investment environment and government engagement, one or all of these strategies might be pursued.
